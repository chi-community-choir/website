# About This Piece

"The Seal Lullaby" was commissioned for the animated film *Kung Fu Panda* but ultimately went unused. Despite this, it has become one of Eric Whitacre's most beloved choral works.

The piece features:
- Gentle, flowing melodic lines
- Lush harmonic progressions
- Text about a mother seal singing to her pup
- Accessible vocal ranges for all parts

## Lyrics

*Oh! Hush thee, my baby, the night is behind us,*
*And black are the waters that sparkled so green.*
*The moon, o'er the combers, looks downward to find us*
*At rest in the hollows that rustle between.*
